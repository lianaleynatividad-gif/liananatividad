Pancake Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 tablespoons melted butter or oil
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract (optional)

Instructions

  1. In one bowl, combine the flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt. Mix well.
  2. In another bowl, beat the egg, then add milk, melted butter (or oil), and vanilla extract.
  3. Pour the wet mixture into the dry ingredients. Stir gently until just combined. (Don’t overmix — small lumps are okay.)
  4. Place a non-stick pan or skillet over medium heat. Lightly grease it with butter or oil.
  5. Pour about 1/4 cup of batter for each pancake.
  6. Cook until bubbles appear on top and the edges look dry (about 1–2 minutes).
  7. Flip and cook the other side until golden brown.
  8. Stack pancakes on a plate and serve warm.
