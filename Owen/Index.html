About Me

Hi! I’m Liana Ley A. Natividad, an IT student at Occidental Mindoro State College, SJ Campus, and I live in Magsaysay, Occidental Mindoro. I love exploring creative hobbies like playing instruments, singing, dancing, and cooking. In my free time, I enjoy reading Wattpad stories, discovering new things, and connecting with people. I’d describe myself as friendly, curious, and determined, always eager to learn and grow. Music is a big part of my life—Enhypen is my favorite boy group, and I know all their songs, but I also enjoy listening to uplifting Christian music. When I’m alone, I find peace in my room while enjoying my favorite foods; it’s my little sanctuary. I live by the belief that "If others can do it, I can too," and I know that with determination, effort, and courage, I can achieve anything I set my mind to.
